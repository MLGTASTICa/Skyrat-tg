29 March 2021

Azarak updated:

  • fixed tail related moodlets

Gandalf2k15 updated:

  • Fixes CME define maths causing them to last for double the time they were meant to.
  • Reduced the amount of time CME's last.
  • Reduced the chance for CME's to spawn.
  • CME's now drop a random loot item when neutralized.
  • CME's no longer spawn a fire when neutralized, unless they're extreme.
  • Civil Dispute Officers! Check https://github.com/Skyrat-SS13/Skyrat-tg/pull/4205
  • 2 new guns to the security sidearm token!
  • You can now select what scream your species has, check it out in the character creator!
  • Donators now get some cool custom screams, check them out!
  • The standard security baton has been buffed and renamed. They also no longer deal lethal damage when you whap people with them.
  • A new stunstick is available from cargo.

Guillaume Prata updated:

  • After investigation. NT found out that Plasmaman detectives were being supplied with "non-Bangproof" headsets to decrease expenses because "The bonehead's helmet protects their bone ears anyway." This misunderstanding on how the helmets work has been clarified and the Quartermaster involved was promptly fired.

RatFromTheJungle updated:

  • Cargo can now properly order fuel for their exo-drones, at a 'steep' price.

SkyratBot updated:

  • Corrected the span and a typo when a pAI gets emagged.
  • delta station distro and waste no longer mix
  • Jungle fevered monkeys are once again dexterous.
  • refactored create_casing_pellets to not sleep
  • tgui tooltips have been moved to a library that should fix them going off the edges of the screen.
  • RPDs can now apply the unwrench upgrade by interacting with the upgrade disk
  • fixed mech hud's not being removed when leaving the mech.
  • Create Antag now allows for a maximum amount of antags to be selected for some options.

SpaceVampire updated:

  • Job Overflow Station Trait.
